Find yourself outside.

Vancouver Park is located in the northwestern part of Vancouver, around the North River, a clear stream with great natural beauty and variety. Surrounded by mountains that show their beauty from season to season, families, groups, and circles can enjoy camping, hiking, barbecuing, playing in the river, fishing, and other fun activities in a quiet environment.

Our Services

    With a knowledgeable guide Wild guide, a sturdy pair of boots, and as much as 24 hours of daylight, you'll be all set for an experience unlike any you've had before.

Q & A

  • What is the maximum number of people allowed per campsite?

    At least one member of the party must be 16 years of age or older. A maximum of eight persons including children (15 years of age and younger).

  • How many vehicles are allowed per campsite/camping party?

    A camping party may be allowed a second motor vehicle not designed as an accommodation for camping on the campsite for an additional % of the camping fee (to a maximum of $12/night).

  • How can I make a reservation?

    Please call us. 236-456-7890

  • What is winter camping and where is it available?

    Vancouver park allow walk-in camping to self-sufficient campers and/or have designated areas of the park available for off-season camping.
